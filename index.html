About J.S. Adam

J.S. Adam is "The Crypto Mentor"—a physicist, engineer, and "real-world professor" who teaches from a place of hard-won experience, not just theory.

With a background in physics, J.S. Adam's true education came from the "unfair" lessons of traditional entrepreneurship. After facing multiple business failures and feeling the deep sting of an "unfair" traditional system, his search for a "fair game" led him to cryptocurrency in 2018.

This belief was forged into an unbreakable conviction during a harrowing, near-fatal battle with COVID-19. After four months in the hospital and with only 5% lung function, his traditional health insurance refused to cover the extensive medical bills. It was his crypto investments, which he could access 24/7 with no middleman, that provided the financial freedom and liquidity to pay for his life-saving treatment.

This profound journey of survival, resilience, and faith is chronicled in his first memoir, "Breaths of Hope."

Having experienced both the "unfairness" of the old system and the "sovereignty" of the new, J.S. Adam's mission is to be the mentor he wishes he'd had. His new series, "THE CRYPTO MENTOR," is the culmination of that mission: a "zero-to-pro" guide dedicated to helping you safely navigate the "Wild West" and build real, generational wealth.

He writes under a pseudonym to protect his privacy, but his message—published under the andrewtips.com brand—is for everyone.
