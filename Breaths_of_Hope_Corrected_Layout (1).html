About the Book

"Breaths of Hope" tells the extraordinary true story of J.S. Adam, capturing his unwavering faith and resilience during his battle with COVID-19. From the challenges of isolation to moments of profound hope, this book is a testament to the unyielding power of the human spirit.

Author's Note

J.S. Adam shares his deeply personal journey, reflecting on the bonds of family, faith, and the will to survive. His message is one of hope and inspiration, inviting readers to find light even in the darkest moments.
